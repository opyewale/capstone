Who are we?

We are a group of young researchers working on creating a low-cost, durable and cheap technology for virtual interaction. We are making our foray into animation and motion capture technologies.

Where are we?

We are currently based in Mumbai, India.

What motivates us?

The endless opportunities that come with the prospect of connecting billions of people in an attractive virtual world where people can hangout, work, trade and what not!
