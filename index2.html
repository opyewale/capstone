Our Current technology allows us to do motion capture of a single person within a camera view using both IMU's, which are attached to the person at their elbows, knees and back, needing a total of 5 IMU's currently. We have been able to achieve 95% accuracy of joint dynamic tracking for high speeds, and 98% accuracy for low speeds. This allows us to study any changes in the trajectory of any part of the body, which help in early diagnosis of many diseases which start with minor changes in the motion pattern of the person. This also helps in improving performance in sports by practicing to match optimal motion pattern depending on body type to achieve a better performance.
